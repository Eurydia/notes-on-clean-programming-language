## Operations

$$
\begin{align*}
a\rightarrow{b}\rightarrow{R}
\end{align*}
$$

- $a$, $b$, and $R$ are of a generic type.

**Behavior**: adds $a$ and $b$.

**Declaration**

```
// Language: Clean

where:
    (+) infixl 6 :: T T -> T
```
